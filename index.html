Our Story

Since our humble beginnings in 1978, Jollibee has grown to become the Philippines' largest fast food chain, with over 1,500 stores nationwide and a presence in 17 countries worldwide. Our commitment to serving great-tasting, high-quality food at affordable prices has made us a favorite among Filipinos and food lovers everywhere.

Introduction

From our iconic Chickenjoy – crispy fried chicken that's juicy on the inside – to our flavorful Jolly Spaghetti and mouthwatering Yumburger, our menu offers something for everyone. We also serve a variety of other delicious options, including burgers, fries, pies, breakfast dishes, and desserts, ensuring there's always a tasty treat waiting for you at Jollibee.

Our Values:

At Jollibee, we believe in putting our customers first and delivering exceptional service with a smile. We're dedicated to upholding the highest standards of food quality, cleanliness, and customer satisfaction in every store, every day. Our commitment to excellence has earned us the trust and loyalty of millions of customers around the world.
